Be sure to read this introduction before you start!

You can quit the application anytime by clicking the "Quit" at the right corner!

1. Make Note: Click the link or icon of "Make Note" at the bottom. You can change the font style by clicking the buttons Bold Font to bold the font, Font Type to change the font to Georgia, Font Size to increase the font size from 20px to 30px. Also, you can click +icon to add the icon to the corelate note. Then, you can start typing on the text box on the keyboard and click OK to save the note. If you wnat to rewrite the note, you can click clear. Once you save the note, it will display below Save Notes.

2. Play Video/Music: Click the link or icon Play Video/Music at the bottom. You can click "Search Video/Music" to search the videos or music you want on the Youtube website. You also can click the popular music or videos at the left or right side. It will directly lead you to the website of the revelent music or videos under the specific name.

3. Set Alarm: Click the link or icon "Set Alarm" at the bottom. You can scroll down or up to select the digits of Hours, Minutes and Seconds, then picking the day of the alarm and type the label from keyboard. When you finish, you can click "Save" or "Cancel" to save or cancel your alarm.

4. Online Consult: Click the link or icon "Online Consult" at the bottom. You can click "Click Here to Ask Questions Online" to talk with a doctor online for free. It will lead you to a online website that you can ask queston and get response from online doctor.
